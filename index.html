How it works

  1. Add your cards — tap + and give each card a name, type and starting balance.
  2. Log a payment — tap the big + button, enter the amount, where and when.
  3. Pick a category — shopping, transfers, entertainment, charity and more.
  4. Watch your balances — every entry updates the card and your Trends.

Cloud sync

Sign in to back up and sync your data across devices. Optional — everything works locally without it.

Security

Lock the app with a 4-digit PIN. It's asked each time you open the app on this device.
